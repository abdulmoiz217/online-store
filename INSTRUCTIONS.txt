# Instructions for Using Your Shoe Store

## Step 1: Add Products
1. Open `simple_admin.html` in your browser
2. Fill in the product details:
   - Product Name (required)
   - Price in dollars (required, must be a positive number)
   - Category (required, choose from dropdown)
   - Description (required)
   - Optionally upload an image
3. Click "Add Product"
4. Repeat for all products you want to add

## Step 2: View Products on Your Website
1. Open `index.html` or `products.html` in your browser
2. Your newly added products will appear on the website
3. Products marked as "Sold" in the admin panel will not appear on the main website

## Step 3: Manage Products
1. Go back to `simple_admin.html`
2. To mark a product as sold: click "Mark as Sold" button next to the product
3. To delete a product: click "Delete" button next to the product

## Notes:
- All data is stored in your browser's localStorage
- Clearing browser data will remove all products
- Products will persist between browser sessions
- The main website will show only products that are NOT marked as sold
